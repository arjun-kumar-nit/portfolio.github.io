4. => Study of Enhanced SEP for improving Network lifetime in WSN.

Final Year Project

Project Summary:

  1. It was observed that network lifetime can be improved by considering higher heterogeneity level of nodes. Also the rate of dissipation of energy of nodes is less in 4 level nodes heterogeneity as compared to its lower equivalent.
  2. Simulation results shows that E-SEP has better efficiency as compared to SEP in terms of parameters used. It extends the lifetime and stability of the Network.
  3. There can be significant improvements and enhancements based on the advancements in this research area particularly. Considering the layered network and multi-hop routing technique may show improvement in the Network lifetime.

Technical Stack: MATLAB , Core subject concepts basically Networking
